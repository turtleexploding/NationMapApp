Privacy Policy for Nation Map

Effective Date: 01/03/2025

1. Introduction
Welcome to Nation Map, a Discord bot designed to assist users with geographical data related to Minecraft nations. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines what information the bot collects and how it is handled.

2. Information We Collect
Nation Map does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The bot only processes the necessary data in real-time to provide its functionalities.

However, in the event of an error, the bot may temporarily log error details to diagnose and resolve issues. These logs may include:

- Command parameters

- Error message and stack trace

These logs are not stored permanently and are only used for debugging purposes.

3. How We Use Data
Error logs, when generated, are solely used to improve the bot’s performance and fix issues. No personal data is stored or shared beyond the debugging process.

4. Data Security
We take reasonable measures to ensure that any error logs generated are protected and deleted once the issue is resolved. The bot does not retain or transmit data beyond what is necessary for functionality and troubleshooting.

5. Third-Party Services
Nation Map does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services. All processing occurs within Discord’s environment.

6. User Control
Since Nation Map does not store personal data, there is no need for users to request data deletion. If you have concerns about error logs, you may contact the bot developer for clarification.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Information
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via Discord.

Thank you for using Nation Map!
